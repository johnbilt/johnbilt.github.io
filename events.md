# First TAMS Community Call!

### Thursday, December 4th at 4pm GMT

Join us for the inaugural TAMS Community Call - a brand new monthly gathering for our growing community!

What to expect:
• TAMS Updates - Latest news on events and software releases
• Community Spotlight - Share your TAMS use cases and launches
• Features & Roadmap - fMP4, Auth, and what's coming next
• Voice of the Vendor - The team behind Omakase Player tell us about their TAMS journey
Whether you're just getting started with TAMS or you're a seasoned pro, this is your chance to connect, learn, and shape the future of our community!
